CONVERSION

In maths,conversion is the process of changing the value of one form to another for example converting pounds to kilograms or inches to millimeters. Units are used for measuring length, measuring weight, measuring capacity and measuring temperature.

TYPE OF CONVERTIONS

Here is some example of conversions type and some information about the process :

TYPE OF CONVERSION DESCRIPTION EXAMPLE
Length Converter This length converter is a tool that enables quick conversion between length units. You can use this to convert centimeter to milimeter , inches to millimeters and kilometers to meter.
Temperature Converter A temperature converter is a tool that helps you convert temperatures from one unit of measurement to another. You can use a temperature converter to convert Celsius to Fahrenheit or Kelvin to Celsius.
Volume Converter A volume converter is a tool that helps you convert between different units of volume. You can use it to convert liters to mililitters and mililitters to liters.
Weight Converter This weight converter tool is the best tool for converting between different weight units. You can use it to convert kilograms to grams and grams to kilograms.
